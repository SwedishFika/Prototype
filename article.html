Are email subscriptions really necessary?

Posted 15 april, 2013

Every site that has a newsletter signup should provide an easy way to unsubscribe from the subscription list that correspond to that newsletter.

A nice way is just to add a link at the bottom of the newsletter labeled “Unsubscribe”. But what do we think should happen when you click on that link?

I think that that the link should open the unsubscription web page in my browser where I must confirm my unsubscription. (I could have clicked the link by mistake so it’s nice to provide a confirmation button) When I confirm my unsubscription all I want is just a message that says whether or not I successfully got unsubscribed.
